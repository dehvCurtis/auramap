# AuraMap — Human Design App

AuraMap is a Human Design charting and social platform that helps you discover your unique energetic blueprint. Generate your personalized bodygraph chart, explore your Type, Authority, Profile, and more — all based on your birth data.

## Features

- **Bodygraph Chart** — Generate your complete Human Design chart with all 9 Centers, 64 Gates, and 36 Channels
- **Chart Properties** — Discover your Type, Authority, Profile, Definition, and Incarnation Cross
- **Planetary Activations** — View your conscious and unconscious planetary positions
- **Saved Charts** — Create and manage charts for friends and family
- **Daily Transits** — Track how current planetary positions interact with your design
- **Affirmations** — Receive daily affirmations tailored to your Human Design Type
- **Discovery** — Find and connect with other users based on HD compatibility
- **Social Feed** — Share posts, stories, and chart insights with the community
- **Direct Messaging** — Chat with friends and share charts in real time
- **Quizzes** — Test your Human Design knowledge across multiple categories
- **Gamification** — Earn points, badges, and climb the leaderboard
- **Premium Subscription** — Unlock advanced features and unlimited sharing
